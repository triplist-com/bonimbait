# Bonim Bait - Company Overview

## Mission

Democratize construction knowledge for Israeli homebuilders by making expert video content searchable, summarized, and actionable.

## What We Do

Bonim Bait (bonimbait.com) is a Hebrew knowledge base that aggregates ~900 YouTube videos from construction expert Tomer Chen Rihana (@TomerChenRihana). The platform transforms hours of video content into a searchable, AI-powered resource where homebuilders can quickly find answers about costs, materials, contractors, permits, and construction best practices.

## Target Audience

Individuals building private homes in Israel who need reliable construction knowledge but lack industry experience. These are people navigating the complex process of planning, permitting, budgeting, and managing a home construction project.

## Content Source

- **Channel**: @TomerChenRihana on YouTube
- **Volume**: ~900 videos (~450 hours of content)
- **Topics**: Planning, permits, costs, contractors, materials, insulation, electrical, plumbing, finishes, landscaping, laws, and general construction tips
- **Language**: Hebrew

## Business Model

### Free Tier (Content Discovery)
- Full-text and semantic search across all 900 videos
- AI-generated answers to construction questions (RAG-powered)
- Category browsing and video summaries
- Cost and price information extracted from videos

### Monetization Funnel

```
Free search & AI answers
    --> Cost estimation wizard (free, collects intent)
        --> Upsell: paid plan-based cost estimation service
            --> Upsell: professional consultation booking (Calendly, 30-min intro call)
```

### Revenue Streams

1. **Cost Estimation Service**: Paid plan-based cost estimation using real data from video content and market pricing. Users input their home specs and receive a detailed cost breakdown.
2. **Professional Consultation**: Calendly-booked 30-minute intro calls for personalized guidance on construction projects. Upsold from the cost wizard results page.

## Language & Localization

- All user-facing content is in **Hebrew**
- The site is **RTL-first** (right-to-left)
- Currency: Israeli New Shekel (NIS)
- Legal context: Israeli construction laws and regulations
